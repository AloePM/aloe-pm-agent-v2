# Lease Prep & Signing — Aloe Property Management (Residential)

Take an **approved** applicant from "terms ready" to a **fully executed lease,
cleared ledger, and move-in**, then hand the file to Property Management. Scope
**picks up where `applications-screening` ends** (at approval/terms-ready) — it
does not screen or decide eligibility.

> **Criteria source of truth:** `playbooks/leasing-coordinator.md` → *Screening
> criteria*. Don't re-decide eligibility here. Income standard is **2.5x NET**
> (already verified at screening).

## Step 1 — Send terms for acceptance

- Send the approved applicant the **contract terms**: **rent, deposit, move-in
  date, fees, lease length.** If a **higher security deposit** was required
  (exception), it's added to the terms to accept.
- **Automatic lease terms by default**; the **PM who ran the application signs
  off** (and adds the higher deposit if needed). Pricing/term changes are never
  set unilaterally.
- Watch the **Aptly notifications panel** for acceptance. Terms accepted → app
  moves to the **Move-Ins board.**

## Step 2 — Collect the $1,500 earnest (off-market trigger)

- Collect the **$1,500 earnest / holding deposit BEFORE the lease is signed.**
- Paid in the **Rentvine portal** (a payment **override** is enabled to allow it,
  then re-disabled). It is **applied toward the security deposit** and
  **forfeited if they back out.**
- **Trigger:** an email that the tenant made the deposit payment (or check the
  account) → **take the home off the market.**

## Step 3 — Build & e-sign the lease (Aptly)

- **"Create Lease" in Aptly pushes application data Aptly → Rentvine — it is not
  the act of writing the lease.**
- Build the lease in **Aptly**: set the title, **remove the emergency contact**,
  verify **rent, RBP, deposit, pet, cleaning, admin** → **confirm.**
- **The lease is e-signed in Aptly, and every adult 18+ on the lease signs.**
- **Fully executed** is confirmed when the lease **auto-transfers Aptly →
  Rentvine** after confirmation.

## Step 4 — Rentvine move-in setup (Office)

Set move-in charges in Rentvine, dated to the **move-in date**:
- **Admin $250** · **Cleaning $500** (non-refundable) · **Pet $250/pet**
  (non-refundable) · **Security Deposit** (varies) · **prorated rent** if move-in
  isn't the 1st · **RBP $15** if move-in is **on/after the 15th** · recurring
  **Rent + RBP** · **NSF $75** policy.
- **Send portal invitations.**
- (The earnest payment override from Step 2 is re-disabled after.)

## Step 5 — Funds before keys, then access

- **The FULL move-in ledger must be paid and cleared to $0 before any key/access
  is released** — earnest, first month, deposit balance, and all move-in charges.
- Once cleared, **release the lockbox/keypad access code at move-in** (consistent
  with self-serve access).

## Step 6 — Move-in inspection & handoff

- **Two move-in walkthroughs:** **Aloe completes its own** move-in walkthrough,
  and the **tenant completes their own** when they move in. File both to the
  property record.
- **Handoff:** once keys/access are released and the tenant has moved in, the
  **leasing file closes** and **Property Management owns the ongoing
  relationship.**
- **File the signed lease as a PDF** per CLAUDE.md naming —
  `PropertyName_Lease_YYYY-MM-DD.pdf` — in the **`Leases/`** folder.

## What good output looks like

A clean, fully-executed move-in:
- $1,500 earnest collected → **home off-market**; all adults **e-signed in
  Aptly**; lease **auto-transferred to Rentvine**; Rentvine charges + portal
  set up; **ledger cleared to $0**; access code released at move-in; **both**
  move-in walkthroughs done; **signed lease filed as PDF** in `Leases/`; leasing
  file **closed** and handed to Property Management.
- Every step logged; PM sign-off on terms; nothing decided on a guess.

## This skill NEVER

- **Never releases keys/access before the full move-in ledger is cleared to $0.**
- **Never sets terms, concessions, or pricing outside the written lease, and
  never makes verbal promises** (holds, pre-move-in repairs, terms) — automatic
  terms by default; exceptions are PM/Owner-approved, never promised.
- **Never executes a lease without every adult 18+ signing.**
- **Never mishandles funds or applicant data** — handle deposits/charges
  correctly; protect personal and financial information.
- **Never varies process or terms by protected class** — fair housing is
  non-negotiable.

## Commercial

Residential only. **Commercial gets its own playbook** — don't apply these rules
to a commercial lease.
