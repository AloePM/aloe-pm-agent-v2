# Lease Violations & Fines — Aloe Property Management (Resident Coordinator)

Violations come from **HOA, City, missed air-filter appointments, or
inspections.** Every one starts with an **Aptly card**; handling then depends on
the **type** — and a **first-offense landscaping violation is a warning only**,
never a cure.

## Step 1 — Always create the Aptly card

Create a card in **Aptly** with the **property address** and the **type of
violation.** Fill out the violation type — **automations start from there.**
Never skip this step.

## Handling by violation type

### Unauthorized pet
If pets are **permitted on the property**: send the **"Unauthorized Pet on
Premises – Action Required"** email (alerts the tenant the pet was observed at
inspection), **send a cure notice**, add the **$75 fee**, and have them fill out
the **Pet Acceptance form.** If approved → **$500 non-refundable pet acceptance
fee + pet addendum.** (See the **pets** skill.)

### Landscaping — FIRST offense
**First, confirm landscaping is the tenant's responsibility.** Check the Rentvine
**"Included Services"** field on the property — landscaping is marked either
**"Tenant Responsibility"** or **"Owner Responsibility."** If it's marked **"Owner
Responsibility,"** it is **included and covered** and the violation is **not the
tenant's** — **route it to the landscaping vendor or notify the owner**, and do
**not** warn or fine the tenant.

If landscaping is marked **"Tenant Responsibility":** **warning only** on a first
offense — send a **warning email** and **note on the tenant's Rentvine page**
that the warning was sent. **Any further incident → cure notice.** Never issue a
cure on a first-offense landscaping violation.

### Smoking
**Fine per the lease.** Smoking is prohibited in the premises — **includes vaping
and medical/recreational marijuana.** If found smoking inside: **minimum $500
liquidated damages + $100 Odor Cleanup Admin Charge.** Document the finding
before charging.

### Missed air-filter appointment
**Charge the missed-appointment fee** and **document in Rentvine.**

## Posting a cure notice (serious or repetitive violations)

1. **Create the cure notice** — templates in **PandaDoc or Aptly.** It **must be
   dated the actual date posted.** **Post it under the Lease page in Rentvine.**
2. **Place the fee on the tenant ledger:**
   - **$5** — HOA warnings or violations.
   - **$75 cure notice fee** — all other violations (lease violation, non-pay,
     unauthorized pet).

## Documentation

Document everything in **Rentvine** — warnings on the tenant page, cure notices
on the **Lease page** — and keep the **Aptly card** updated.

## What good output looks like

- **Aptly card created** with address + violation type (automations triggered).
- The **right path for the type** — landscaping first offense = **warning only**;
  repeat/serious = cure notice.
- Cure notice **dated the actual posting date** and **posted on the Lease page**,
  with the correct fee (**$5** HOA / **$75** other).
- Smoking fine (**$500 + $100**) charged **only with documentation.**
- Everything documented in Rentvine; the Aptly card kept current.

## This skill NEVER

- **Never posts a cure notice without dating it the actual posting date.**
- **Never charges a smoking fine without documentation.**
- **Never skips creating the Aptly card.**
- **Never issues a cure for a first-offense landscaping violation — warning
  only.**
- **Never issues a landscaping violation to a tenant without first checking the
  Rentvine "Included Services" field** — if landscaping is marked "Owner
  Responsibility," it's covered and not the tenant's.
