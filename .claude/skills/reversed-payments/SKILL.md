# Reversed Payments / Prepaid Rent — Aloe Property Management (Resident Coordinator)

**Scope — read this first.** This procedure applies **only when a tenant
proactively reaches out** saying their payment is going to reverse (or just did)
and they **want to pay now** — to avoid late fees and not wait for the reversal
to finish processing. The tenant initiates; they want to pay the full amount
including fees right away. **This is not a general or automatic reversed-payment
workflow.**

To make that possible, we add a temporary **"Prepaid Rent"** charge so the full
amount (rent + all fees) is payable in the portal immediately, then remove it
once their payment clears.

## The Prepaid Rent charge

A temporary **"Prepaid Rent"** charge that totals:
- **Rent** amount
- **RBP** (Resident Benefit Package)
- **Dishonored funds fee**
- **Late fees** equal to the number of days since the payment was due (calculated
  to the day they are paying)

## Steps

1. **Tenant proactively contacts us** — their payment is going to reverse (Rentvine
   will/does show a **dishonored payment**) and they want to pay now.
2. **Add the temporary "Prepaid Rent" charge** = rent + RBP + dishonored funds fee
   + late fees calculated to the day they are paying.
3. **Tell the tenant the total now due**, including all fees, so they can pay it.
4. **Tenant makes the payment in the portal and notifies us.**
5. **Once the payment is confirmed cleared in Rentvine, remove the temporary
   Prepaid Rent charges.**
6. **Document everything on the Rentvine lease page.**

## What good output looks like

- Triggered by a **tenant-initiated** call about a payment that will reverse —
  not applied automatically to every dishonored payment.
- **Prepaid Rent temp charge** built correctly (rent + RBP + dishonored funds fee
  + late fees to the pay date) so the tenant can pay in full immediately.
- Tenant told the **exact total due**; payment made in the portal.
- Temp charge **removed only after payment is confirmed cleared in Rentvine.**
- Every step **documented on the Rentvine lease page.**

## This skill NEVER

- **Never removes the Prepaid Rent charges before confirming the payment cleared
  in Rentvine.**
- **Never waives the dishonored funds fee without Property Manager approval.**
- **Never lets a reversed payment sit without contacting the tenant the same
  day.**
