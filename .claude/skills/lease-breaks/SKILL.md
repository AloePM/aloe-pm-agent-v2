# Lease Breaks — Aloe Property Management (Resident Coordinator)

A lease break is a tenant **ending their lease early.** Two rules govern every
case: **check the actual lease first** (terms vary by version), and **the fee is
paid before the break and move-out — never after.** Be warm and professional;
never quote terms from memory.

## Step 1 — Check the lease terms (they vary)

Read the **actual lease on the property** before saying anything — don't assume:

- **Current leases:** **30 days notice** · lease break penalty = **2 months rent
  + $250 admin fee.**
- **Some older leases:** the option of **$750 + rent until the home is
  re-rented**, OR **2 months lease break only.**
- **Taken-over leases:** terms are **unknown until verified** — confirm on the
  actual lease before discussing anything.

## Step 2 — Notice, then fee FIRST

- On notice, the tenant must **pay the lease break fee before breaking the lease
  and moving out.**
- Place a charge of **2 months rent** on the ledger under the **"Lease Break Fee"
  account** (plus the **$250 admin fee** on current leases).
- **Once the lease break fee is paid, the tenant has 30 days from that point to
  vacate.**
- **One more month of rent is due** during that 30-day window → **3 months total**
  (the 2-month lease break fee + 1 month rent).

### Example
> Tenant gives notice **Feb 28**. Tenant pays **March rent + the 2-month lease
> break fee.** The fee is now paid, so the 30-day clock starts and they vacate
> within 30 days.

## Authority

- The coordinator **never waives or reduces** the lease break fee — that's a
  **PM decision**; escalate the request, promise nothing.
- **No move-out date is set until the fee is confirmed paid.**
- Only discuss lease break terms with the **leaseholder.**

## Documentation

Document **everything in Rentvine on the lease page** — notice date, lease
version/terms verified, fee charged, fee paid, and the move-out date.

## What good output looks like

- Lease terms **verified on the actual lease** (version identified, taken-over
  leases confirmed).
- Lease break fee **charged under the "Lease Break Fee" account** and **paid
  before** any move-out date is set.
- The **3-month math** (2-month fee + 1 month rent) explained to the tenant.
- Any waiver/reduction request **escalated to the PM.**
- Everything **documented in Rentvine on the lease page.**

## This skill NEVER

- **Never confirms a lease break without first checking the actual lease terms.**
- **Never waives or reduces the lease break fee without Property Manager
  approval.**
- **Never sets a move-out date without confirming the fee is paid first.**
- **Never discusses lease break terms with anyone other than the leaseholder.**
